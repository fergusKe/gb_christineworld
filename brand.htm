•         1929年，Christine於法國第二大城市馬賽成立。起初，只是一家小型家具廠，直到1990年才開始製作手工床墊。當Christine只是一家小型家具廠時就一直堅持著「環保、健康、高品質」的理念，給與顧客最好的產品，Christine延續此項理念以製作床墊。

            1997年，隨著有機棉的誕生與量產，Christine開始採用有機棉以製造床墊，也因此Christine的床墊就以「有機棉」著名。Christine所採用的有機棉是來自土耳其純天然有機棉，並經過荷蘭Control Union認證，完全符合GOTS全球有機棉紡織標準，種植過程不灑農藥以保護地球，使溫室氣體降低、幫助溫室效應下降。

            Christine使用德國原裝AGRO獨立筒彈簧，AGRO彈簧最多可使用4個以上的電極熱處理，以消除在捲繞過程中鐵線產生的張力，感覺更舒適，預壓縮可確保彈簧在裝袋時更加牢靠，這可確保彈簧在使用數年後仍不會有"下陷"的風險。

            1929, Christine was founded in Marseille, the second large city of France. At first, it is only a small furniture factory until 1990 began making handmade mattresses. Christine has always insisted on “eco-friendly, healthy and high-quality” concept when it was just a small furniture factory in order to provide the best sleeping product to customers. Christine continues of this idea to produce mattresses.

            In 1997, with the birth of organic cotton production, Christine began using organic cotton to manufacture mattresses, and became famous with the usage of organic cotton. The organic cotton is made from Turkey, through the Netherlands’ Control Union Certificated and after full compliance with global organic cotton GOTS standards; planting process does not spill pesticides to protect the Earth, reducing greenhouse gases and help decrease the greenhouse effect.
